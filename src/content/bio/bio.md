## About

I lead AI and R&D at BJET, a Ukrainian Odoo consulting and product company. My three-person team owns the company's AI infrastructure, production RAG systems, Claude Code adoption, and SDD (Software Design Document) methodology.

I came to engineering through architecture and BIM management, moved into Odoo development at Kitworks, and grew into a Tech Lead role at BJET during a regression crisis that needed someone willing to own the system end-to-end. That pattern — system responsibility leading to new complexity — has shaped how I work ever since.

My current focus is the intersection of AI workflows and ERP development: building the tools, standards, and infrastructure that let Odoo teams ship faster without lowering the bar on quality. I work in Python (FastAPI, LlamaIndex), Odoo CE/EE 17+, Postgres with pgvector, and the full Claude Code stack.

Long-term, I'm building toward a small, high-caliber consulting practice — three to five trusted people, direct clients, specific industry verticals.
